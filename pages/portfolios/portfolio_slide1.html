Primary and Community Care Services (PCCS)

Details
  • Category: Android/iOS App
  • Date: 2016
  • Languages: HTML, CSS, JS, PHP
  • Technologies: Bootstrap, jQuery, PhoneGap
Description
A responsive web application and an iOS/Android Application was developed for PCCS to integrate with their content management system to allow real time access to health providers in New South Wales. Scope of work included: Automation of real time capture of hospitals emergency waiting times using a government provided API, Google maps navigation, dynamic tabbing feature to support future services and the ability to favourite health providers and list them based on closest location to user.
